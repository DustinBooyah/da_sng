Sealcoating

Sealcoating

SnG Pavement Marking is ready to handle any public or private thermo marking job. We are D.O.T certified for all thermoplastic roadwork in Gainesville & Florida.

Thermoplastic is heated to around 400 degrees and consists of: pigments, binders and glass beads that form a durable, longer lasting product with nighttime visibility.

For high traffic areas and challenging climates, thermoplastic markings that SnG in Gainesville, FL are a smart long-term investment. Their high resistance to heat, harsh weather, oil, and grease offers excellent protection against cracking during extreme temperature shifts and extends the lifespan of your application. This durability plus the superior reflectivity of thermoplastic marking adds up to an effective, cost-efficient safety and traffic control solution. All traffic & specialty markings are available.
